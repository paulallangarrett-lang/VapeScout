The UK Disposable Vape Ban Explained

On 1 June 2025, the UK government implemented a complete ban on the sale of disposable vapes across England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland. This means products like Elf Bar, Lost Mary, SKE Crystal Bar, and other single-use vapes can no longer be legally sold in the UK.

What's Banned?

  • All single-use/disposable vape devices
  • Pre-filled non-rechargeable devices
  • Any vape designed to be thrown away after use

What's Still Legal?

  • Refillable pod systems (like XROS, Caliburn)
  • Bottled e-liquids (including nic salts)
  • Rechargeable devices with replaceable pods/coils
  • Box mods and tank systems

The ban was introduced primarily for environmental reasons ‚Äì an estimated 5 million disposable vapes were being thrown away every week in the UK, creating significant electronic waste and pollution concerns.

The good news? You can still get exactly the same vaping experience with a refillable device and bottled e-liquid. In fact, you'll save significant money and have access to an even wider range of flavours.

Step-by-Step: Making the Switch

1

Buy Your Starter Kit

Get a pod device (we recommend XROS 4 or Caliburn G3), a 2-pack of replacement pods, and 2-3 bottles of nic salt e-liquid in flavours you like. Total cost: approximately ¬£30-40.

2

Charge Your Device

Connect the USB-C cable and fully charge before first use. Most devices take 45-90 minutes to fully charge. The LED will indicate when charging is complete.

3

Fill Your Pod

Remove the pod from the device. Open the rubber fill port (usually on the side). Slowly add e-liquid, being careful not to overfill past the max line or get liquid in the centre airflow tube.

4

Wait 5-10 Minutes

Important: Let the filled pod sit for 5-10 minutes before vaping. This allows the cotton wick to fully saturate with e-liquid. Vaping too soon can burn the coil and ruin the pod.

5

Insert Pod and Vape

Push the pod into the device until it clicks. Draw on the mouthpiece like you would a disposable (most pods are draw-activated). If your device has a button, press while drawing.

6

Maintain Your Device

Top up your pod when e-liquid runs low (don't let it run dry). Replace the pod/coil every 1-2 weeks or when flavour tastes burnt. Clean the contacts occasionally with a tissue.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå

Not Priming the Coil

Vaping immediately after filling can burn the dry cotton wick. Always wait 5-10 minutes after filling a new pod.

‚ùå

Choosing the Wrong Nicotine Strength

If you're used to disposables, start with 20mg nic salts. Lower strengths won't satisfy cravings and may lead you back to disposables.

‚ùå

Using Nic Salts in Sub-Ohm Devices

High-strength nic salts (10-20mg) are designed for low-power pod systems. Using them in powerful sub-ohm tanks can cause nicotine overdose symptoms.

‚ùå

Letting the Pod Run Dry

Keep your pod topped up. Vaping with low e-liquid levels can burn the coil and create a harsh, burnt taste.

‚ùå

Giving Up Too Quickly

The first day or two might feel slightly different from disposables. Give yourself a few days to adjust ‚Äì most people prefer refillables once they're used to them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will refillable vapes taste the same as my disposable?

Yes, if you use the right e-liquid. Elfliq is made by the same company as Elf Bar and contains identical flavours. MaryLiq is the official Lost Mary e-liquid with the same recipes. The flavour experience should be virtually identical.

How long does a pod/coil last?

A typical pod or coil lasts 1-2 weeks with average use, though this varies based on how much you vape and the sweetness of your e-liquid. Sweet flavours tend to reduce coil life. You'll know it's time to change when the flavour tastes muted or burnt.

Is refillable vaping harder than using disposables?

There's a small learning curve, but it's very simple once you've done it once. The main differences are: filling the pod (takes 30 seconds), charging the device (like charging a phone), and changing the pod/coil every 1-2 weeks. Most people find it very easy after the first day.

What nicotine strength should I choose?

Most disposables contain 20mg nicotine, so start with 20mg nic salts for the closest experience. If you find it too strong, try 10mg. Never use 20mg nic salts in a high-power sub-ohm device ‚Äì they're designed for low-power pod systems only.

Can I still buy disposables online from abroad?

While it may be technically possible to purchase disposables from overseas retailers, we don't recommend it. Products may not meet UK safety standards, could be counterfeit, and importing them may have legal implications. Refillable alternatives offer a better, safer, and more cost-effective solution.

How much will I really save?

Based on using one disposable per day (common usage), you'd spend approximately ¬£1,500/year on disposables. With refillables, you'd spend approximately ¬£200-300/year on e-liquid plus ¬£50-100 on replacement pods and occasional device upgrades. That's a saving of over ¬£1,000 per year for the average vaper.
