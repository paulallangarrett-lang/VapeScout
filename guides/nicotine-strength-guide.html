Understanding Nicotine Strengths

Nicotine strength is measured in milligrams per millilitre (mg/ml), often written simply as "mg". The higher the number, the more nicotine per puff. Getting the right strength is crucial ‚Äì too low and you'll still crave cigarettes, too high and you might feel dizzy or nauseous.

Switching from Cigarettes

Your starting strength depends on how much you smoke:

Recommended Starting Strengths

  • 1-5 cigarettes/day: 5-10mg nic salt or 3-6mg freebase
  • 5-10 cigarettes/day: 10-20mg nic salt or 6-12mg freebase
  • 10-20 cigarettes/day: 20mg nic salt or 12-18mg freebase
  • 20+ cigarettes/day: 20mg nic salt (maximum UK strength)

Pro tip: It's better to start too high than too low. You can always reduce, but if the nicotine is too weak, you'll go back to cigarettes.

Already Vaping & Want to Reduce

If you're looking to lower your nicotine intake:

  • Drop gradually: 20mg ‚Üí 10mg ‚Üí 5mg ‚Üí 0mg
  • Give each level 2-4 weeks before stepping down
  • Consider mixing: Blend 20mg and 10mg bottles for 15mg
  • Don't rush: Slow reduction is more sustainable

Signs Your Nicotine Strength is Wrong

Too Low (Need Higher Strength)

  • Still craving cigarettes
  • Vaping constantly but never satisfied
  • Chain vaping (puff after puff)
  • E-liquid running out very quickly

Too High (Need Lower Strength)

  • Feeling dizzy or lightheaded
  • Headaches after vaping
  • Nausea or feeling sick
  • Heart racing
  • Hiccups (less common)

Frequently Asked Questions

What strength is closest to a cigarette?

20mg nic salt in a pod system provides the closest experience to smoking a cigarette in terms of nicotine satisfaction and throat feel. It won't taste like a cigarette (even tobacco flavours taste different), but the nicotine hit is comparable.

Is 20mg too strong?

For ex-smokers and disposable users, 20mg nic salt is usually perfect. It might feel strong at first, but your body adjusts. If you experience dizziness or nausea, try 10mg instead. For sub-ohm vaping, 20mg would be far too strong ‚Äì stick to 3-6mg.

Can I mix different nicotine strengths?

Yes! Mixing equal parts 20mg and 10mg gives you 15mg. Mixing 20mg and 0mg gives you 10mg. This is useful for finding your perfect strength or gradually reducing.

Why can't I buy higher than 20mg in the UK?

UK/EU TPD regulations cap nicotine e-liquid at 20mg/ml for safety reasons. This is considered sufficient for most smokers to quit. Higher strengths are available in some other countries but cannot legally be sold in the UK.
