What to Look For in a Pod Kit

If you're switching from disposables or cigarettes, here's what matters most:

1. Draw Activation

Draw-activated pods fire when you inhale ‚Äì just like a disposable or cigarette. No buttons to press. This is ideal for beginners. Some kits offer both draw and button activation for flexibility.

2. MTL (Mouth-to-Lung) Draw

For nic salts and ex-smokers, you want a tight MTL draw that mimics cigarette inhalation. Look for pods with 1.0 ohm+ coils and adjustable airflow so you can fine-tune the tightness.

3. Battery Life

A 900-1200mAh battery will last most users a full day. Smaller batteries (under 500mAh) may need midday charging. All modern pod kits use USB-C fast charging.

4. Pod Capacity

UK TPD regulations limit pod capacity to 2ml. Most pods hold 1.8-2ml. One full pod with 20mg nic salt lasts roughly as long as 3-5 disposable vapes.

5. Coil Availability & Cost

Check that replacement pods/coils are readily available in the UK. Popular brands like Caliburn, XROS and Argus have coils stocked everywhere. Budget ¬£2-4 per coil, lasting 1-2 weeks each.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do pod coils last?

Typically 1-2 weeks depending on how much you vape and what e-liquid you use. Sweet flavours gunk up coils faster. You'll know it's time to change when the flavour tastes burnt or muted.

Can I use any e-liquid in a pod kit?

Pod kits work best with nic salts or 50/50 e-liquids. High VG e-liquids (70VG+) are too thick and can cause dry hits. Stick to liquids designed for MTL vaping.

Which pod kit is closest to an Elf Bar?

The Voopoo Argus P2 is the closest experience ‚Äì it's draw-activated, compact, and delivers a similar tight draw. Pair it with Elfliq e-liquids for the same flavours.

How much do replacement pods/coils cost?

Replacement coils typically cost ¬£2-4 each, or ¬£8-12 for a pack of 4-5. This works out far cheaper than disposables ‚Äì one ¬£3 coil lasts as long as 5-10 disposables.
