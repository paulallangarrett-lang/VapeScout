Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

VapeScout UK uses cookies for several purposes:

  • To remember your age verification
  • To remember your cookie preferences
  • To understand how you use our website
  • To improve your browsing experience
  • To enable affiliate tracking

When you click through to a retailer website, they may set their own cookies. These cookies are subject to the third party's own cookie policy. We have no control over these cookies.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Delete all cookies from your browser
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Allow cookies only from certain websites
  • Block third-party cookies

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

6. What Happens If You Disable Cookies?

If you disable cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly:

  • You will need to verify your age every time you visit
  • Your preferences will not be remembered
  • Affiliate tracking may not work (you can still make purchases, but we may not receive commission)

7. Do Not Track

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activity tracked. Our website currently does not respond to Do Not Track signals.
