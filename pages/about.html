About VapeScout UK

We're on a mission to help UK vapers find the best prices on e-liquids. No more overpaying for your favourite vape juice.

Our Story

VapeScout UK was founded in 2026 with a simple observation: the same e-liquid can cost £1.99 at one shop and £4.50 at another. That's a price difference of over 125% for the exact same product.

As vapers ourselves, we found it frustrating to visit multiple websites just to find a fair price. So we built the tool we wished existed – a simple, fast way to compare e-liquid prices across dozens of UK retailers.

What We Do

VapeScout UK is a price comparison website dedicated to e-liquids and vaping products. We aggregate prices from over 50 UK vape shops, making it easy to find the best deal in seconds.

Our service includes:

  • Real-time price comparison across 50+ retailers
  • Multi-buy deal calculations (we show you the true price per bottle)
  • Price history tracking
  • Price drop alerts
  • Helpful guides for new vapers and those switching from disposables

How We Make Money

VapeScout UK is free to use. We make money through affiliate partnerships with retailers. When you click through to a retailer and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission.

Importantly:

  • The price you pay is the same whether you use our links or not
  • Our comparison results are ordered by price, not by commission
  • We include retailers even if we don't have an affiliate relationship with them

For full details, see our Affiliate Disclosure.

Who We Compare
