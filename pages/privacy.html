Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

VapeScout ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website vapescout.co.uk (the "Site").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Subscribe to our price alert newsletter
  • Contact us via our contact form
  • Confirm your age through our age verification gate

This information may include your email address and any other information you choose to provide.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and your visit, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymised where possible)
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information about the cookies we use.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services: Displaying price comparisons and helping you find the best deals
  • To send price alerts: If you subscribe, we'll email you when prices drop on products you're interested in
  • To improve our Site: Understanding how visitors use our Site helps us improve the user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations: Including age verification requirements for vaping product information
  • To respond to enquiries: If you contact us, we'll use your information to respond
  • For analytics: To understand traffic patterns and Site performance

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you subscribe to price alerts or accept cookies, you provide consent
  • Legitimate interests: To improve our services and understand how visitors use our Site
  • Legal obligation: To comply with age verification requirements for vaping-related content

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third parties who help us operate our Site (e.g., hosting providers, email service providers, analytics providers)
  • Affiliate networks: When you click through to a retailer, the affiliate network (e.g., Awin) may receive limited information to track the referral. This does not include your email address or personal details
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Business transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition

5. Third-Party Services

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites (retailers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any website you visit.

5.1 Analytics Services

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the Site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our Site. We use this information to improve our Site. Google Analytics does not collect your name or other identifying information.

5.2 Affiliate Networks

When you click on a retailer link, you may be redirected through an affiliate network (such as Awin). These networks use cookies to track referrals and may collect:

  • The fact that you clicked a link from VapeScout
  • Whether you made a purchase
  • The general value of your purchase (not product details)

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Email addresses (price alerts): Until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Age verification status: Stored locally on your device for 30 days
  • Analytics data: Anonymised and retained for up to 26 months
  • Contact form submissions: For up to 2 years unless earlier deletion is requested

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing: Request limitation of how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 11.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is intended for adults aged 18 and over only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected personal information from someone under 18, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe we have collected information from someone under 18, please contact us.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK where our service providers are located. Where we transfer data outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place as required by UK data protection law.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vapescout.co.uk
  • Contact form: Contact page

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe we have not handled your personal information properly. Visit ico.org.uk for more information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
