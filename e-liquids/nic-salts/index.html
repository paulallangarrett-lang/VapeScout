What Are Nic Salts?

Nicotine salts (nic salts) are a form of nicotine that occurs naturally in tobacco leaves. Unlike freebase nicotine (used in most traditional e-liquids), nic salts are combined with benzoic acid which allows for a much smoother vaping experience at higher nicotine strengths.

Why Choose Nic Salts?

  • Smoother throat hit: Vape 20mg nicotine without harshness
  • Faster nicotine absorption: Satisfies cravings quicker, similar to smoking
  • Better for pod devices: Designed for low-power MTL (mouth-to-lung) vaping
  • Same flavours as disposables: Elfliq, MaryLiq, and SKE Crystal recreate popular disposable flavours
  • More cost-effective: 10ml bottle = equivalent of 5+ disposables at a fraction of the cost

Nic Salt FAQs

What nicotine strength nic salt should I choose?

20mg: Heavy smokers (20+ cigarettes/day) or those used to disposable vapes
10mg: Moderate smokers (10-20 cigarettes/day) or stepping down from 20mg
5mg: Light smokers or those reducing their nicotine intake

If you're switching from disposables like Elf Bar or Lost Mary, start with 20mg as this is what most disposables contain.

Can I use nic salts in any vape device?

Nic salts are designed for low-power pod systems and MTL (mouth-to-lung) devices with coil resistances above 1.0 ohm. They are NOT recommended for high-power sub-ohm devices as the high nicotine content combined with large vapour production could cause nicotine overdose symptoms (nausea, dizziness).

Popular devices for nic salts include: Vaporesso XROS, Uwell Caliburn, Voopoo Argus, and SMOK Nord.

How long does a 10ml bottle of nic salt last?

This depends on how much you vape, but on average a 10ml bottle lasts 3-5 days for a typical vaper. A 10ml bottle provides approximately 3,000 puffs - equivalent to 5 disposable vapes.

Are nic salts safer than cigarettes?

According to Public Health England (now Office for Health Improvement and Disparities), vaping is at least 95% less harmful than smoking cigarettes. Nic salts contain nicotine (which is addictive) but don't contain the tar, carbon monoxide, and thousands of other harmful chemicals found in cigarette smoke.

While not completely risk-free, switching from smoking to vaping significantly reduces your exposure to harmful substances.

What's the difference between Elfliq and Elf Bar?

Elfliq is the official bottled e-liquid range from the makers of Elf Bar. The flavours are identical to Elf Bar disposables, but come in 10ml bottles that you can use in a refillable pod device. This is much more cost-effective and environmentally friendly than buying disposables.
