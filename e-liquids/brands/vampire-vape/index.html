About Vampire Vape

Vampire Vape is a British e-liquid company based in Accrington, Lancashire. Founded in 2012, they've grown to become one of the UK's most recognised vape brands, famous for creating the legendary Heisenberg and Pinkman flavours.

All Vampire Vape e-liquids are manufactured in the UK using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients. They operate from a state-of-the-art facility and follow strict quality control processes to ensure consistency batch after batch.

The brand has won numerous awards including "Best E-Liquid Manufacturer" at the Vaping Industry Awards. Heisenberg alone has been voted the UK's favourite e-liquid multiple years in a row.

Vampire Vape FAQs

What flavour is Heisenberg?

Heisenberg is Vampire Vape's signature mystery flavour - a complex blend that's famously hard to describe. Most people taste a mix of sweet berries, cool menthol, and a hint of aniseed. The exact recipe is a closely guarded secret, which is part of what makes it so iconic. It's one of the UK's best-selling e-liquids and has won multiple awards.

What's the difference between Heisenberg and Pinkman?

Heisenberg has a cool, menthol finish with berry and aniseed notes, while Pinkman is a sweeter, fruitier blend with red berry, grapefruit, and blackcurrant flavours without the menthol kick. If you like cooling sensations, go for Heisenberg. If you prefer pure fruit flavour, try Pinkman.

Is Vampire Vape a UK company?

Yes, Vampire Vape is a British company based in Accrington, Lancashire. All their e-liquids are made in the UK using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients. They've been making e-liquids since 2012 and have grown to become one of the country's most recognised vape brands.

What nicotine strengths are available?

Vampire Vape offers multiple formats: Nic Salts in 10mg and 20mg, 50/50 e-liquids in 0mg, 3mg, 6mg, and 12mg, and Shortfills in 0mg (with space to add nic shots). Most pod users prefer the nic salts, while tank users often choose 50/50 or shortfills.
