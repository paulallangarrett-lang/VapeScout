MaryLiq FAQs

Is MaryLiq the same as Lost Mary?

MaryLiq is made by the same company as Lost Mary and uses identical flavour recipes. The only difference is the format ‚Äì MaryLiq comes in 10ml bottles for refillable devices, while Lost Mary was a disposable. The taste is designed to be identical.

What nicotine strength should I choose?

Most Lost Mary disposables contained 20mg nicotine, so 20mg is the closest match. Choose 10mg if you want a lighter hit or are looking to gradually reduce your nicotine intake.

What device do I need for MaryLiq?

MaryLiq works with any refillable pod system. We recommend the Vaporesso XROS 4, Uwell Caliburn G3, or Voopoo Argus P2 for a similar experience to Lost Mary disposables. See our best pod kits guide.

How long does a bottle of MaryLiq last?

One 10ml bottle provides approximately 3,000 puffs, equivalent to about 5 Lost Mary disposables. For an average user going through one disposable per day, a bottle of MaryLiq lasts 4-5 days.

Yes, significantly cheaper. A Lost Mary disposable cost ¬£5-6 for ~600 puffs. MaryLiq costs ¬£2.99-4.99 for ~3,000 puffs. You can save over ¬£1,000 per year by switching.
