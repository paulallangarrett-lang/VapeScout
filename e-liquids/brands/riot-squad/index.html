About Riot Squad

Riot Squad is a British e-liquid brand known for bold, uncompromising flavours. Based in the UK, they've built a reputation for creating e-liquids that pack maximum flavour punch.

Their BAR EDTN range was specifically designed for vapers transitioning from disposables, offering familiar flavour profiles with Riot Squad's signature intensity. The range has become a favourite among ex-disposable users.

All Riot Squad e-liquids are manufactured in TPD-compliant facilities using premium ingredients.
