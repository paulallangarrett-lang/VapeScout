About Dinner Lady

Dinner Lady is a British e-liquid manufacturer based in Blackburn, Lancashire. Founded in 2016, they quickly rose to international fame with their signature Lemon Tart flavour - now one of the most awarded e-liquids in the world.

The brand takes its name from nostalgic British school dinner desserts, with flavours like Cornflake Tart, Rice Pudding, and Blackberry Crumble evoking memories of childhood treats. Their attention to flavour authenticity has earned them over 35 international awards.

All Dinner Lady e-liquids are manufactured in their state-of-the-art UK facility using pharmaceutical-grade ingredients. They're one of the few brands to achieve ISO 9001 certification for quality management.

Dinner Lady FAQs

What does Lemon Tart taste like?

Lemon Tart delivers an authentic dessert experience - tangy lemon curd balanced with a buttery pastry base and sweet meringue top notes. It's not overly sweet or artificial, which is why it's won so many awards.

Is Dinner Lady a UK company?

Yes, Dinner Lady is proudly British. They're based in Blackburn, Lancashire and manufacture all their e-liquids in a purpose-built, ISO 9001 certified UK facility.

What nicotine strengths are available?

Dinner Lady offers multiple formats: Nic Salts in 10mg and 20mg, 50/50 in 0mg, 3mg, 6mg, 12mg, and 18mg, plus 50ml Shortfills in 0mg with space for nic shots.

Which Dinner Lady flavour is best for beginners?

Lemon Tart is the go-to recommendation - it's universally loved and gives you a taste of why Dinner Lady is so highly regarded. If you prefer fruit over dessert, try Pink Berry from their Fruits range.
