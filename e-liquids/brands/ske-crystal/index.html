SKE Crystal FAQs

Are SKE Crystal nic salts the same as Crystal Bar?

Yes, SKE Crystal nic salts are made by the same company that makes Crystal Bar disposables. They're designed to replicate the exact same flavours you love from Crystal Bar in a refillable e-liquid format. The Blue Razz Lemonade nic salt tastes like the Blue Razz Lemonade Crystal Bar, and so on.

What nicotine strengths do SKE Crystal nic salts come in?

SKE Crystal nic salts are available in 10mg and 20mg nicotine strengths. Most Crystal Bar users find 20mg provides the closest experience to their disposables. If you find 20mg too strong, try 10mg for a smoother vape.

How much can I save switching from Crystal Bar?

You can save around 85-90% compared to buying Crystal Bar disposables. A 10ml bottle of SKE Crystal nic salt costs around ¬£2.49-¬£4.99 and provides roughly 3,000 puffs (equivalent to 5 Crystal Bars). Compare that to spending ¬£25-30 on 5 disposables!

What device should I use with SKE Crystal nic salts?

SKE Crystal nic salts work best in low-power pod systems like the XROS 4, Caliburn G3, or Argus P2. These devices provide the tight draw and smooth nic salt delivery that Crystal Bar users are used to. Avoid high-powered sub-ohm devices.

Will Crystal Bar be banned?

Yes, Crystal Bar and all single-use disposable vapes will be banned in the UK from June 2025. SKE Crystal nic salts will remain legal as they're refillable e-liquids, not disposable devices. This makes them the perfect way to keep enjoying Crystal Bar flavours after the ban.
