About IVG

IVG (I Love VG) is a British e-liquid manufacturer based in Preston, Lancashire. Since 2016, they've been producing premium e-liquids with a focus on bold, satisfying flavours.

Their Bar Favourites range was developed specifically for vapers switching from disposables, offering familiar flavour profiles in a more economical nic salt format. The range has become hugely popular among ex-Elf Bar and Crystal Bar users.
