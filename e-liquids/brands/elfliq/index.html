Elfliq FAQs

Is Elfliq the same as Elf Bar?

Elfliq is made by the same company as Elf Bar and uses identical flavour recipes. The only difference is that Elfliq comes in 10ml bottles for use with refillable devices, while Elf Bar was a disposable device. The taste should be virtually identical.

What nicotine strength should I choose?

Elfliq comes in 10mg and 20mg strengths. Most Elf Bar disposables contained 20mg nicotine, so 20mg is the closest match if you're switching from disposables. Choose 10mg if you want a lighter hit or are looking to reduce nicotine intake.

What device do I need for Elfliq?

Elfliq works with any refillable pod system or MTL device. Popular choices include the Vaporesso XROS, Uwell Caliburn G3, and Voopoo Argus. These give a similar draw to disposables and are easy to use. See our best pod kits guide for recommendations.

How many puffs do I get from one bottle?

One 10ml bottle of Elfliq provides approximately 3,000-4,000 puffs depending on your device and vaping style. That's equivalent to 5-6 Elf Bar disposables, at a fraction of the cost.
