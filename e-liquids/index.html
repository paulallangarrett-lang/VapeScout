Bar Juice 5000 FAQs

Is Bar Juice 5000 the same as Elf Bar?

No, Bar Juice 5000 is not made by Elf Bar. It's an independent UK brand that creates bar-style nic salts inspired by popular disposable flavours. For official Elf Bar flavours, you want Elfliq.

What nicotine strengths are available?

Bar Juice 5000 is available in 10mg and 20mg nicotine strengths. For a similar experience to disposables, choose 20mg. Choose 10mg if you want a lighter hit.

What device should I use with Bar Juice 5000?

Bar Juice 5000 works with any refillable pod system or MTL device. Popular options include the Vaporesso XROS 4, Uwell Caliburn G3, and Voopoo Argus P2.

Where is Bar Juice 5000 made?

Bar Juice 5000 is manufactured in the UK. All products are TPD compliant and meet UK safety standards.
